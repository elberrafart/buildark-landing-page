Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 8, 2026

BuildArk ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website at buildark.dev and the booking platform at bookings.buildark.dev. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

We only collect information you voluntarily provide:

  • Contact form: name, email address, phone number (optional), company name (optional), and your message.
  • Booking form: name, email address, and scheduling preferences when you book a call through our booking platform.

We do not use cookies for tracking, advertising, or analytics on our main website. Our booking platform uses a session cookie solely for admin authentication.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiry or confirm your booking.
  • To send you a one-time confirmation email after you submit the contact form or book a call.

We do not send marketing emails, newsletters, or promotional messages unless you explicitly request them.

Third-Party Services

We use the following services to operate:

  • Resend — to send transactional emails (confirmations and inquiry notifications). Resend Privacy Policy
  • Vercel — to host our websites. Vercel Privacy Policy
  • Zoho — for calendar and meeting scheduling on our booking platform. Zoho Privacy Policy

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with anyone else.

Data Retention

We keep contact form submissions and booking records for as long as needed to fulfill the purpose they were collected for (typically the duration of a project relationship). You can request deletion at any time.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw any consent you have given.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at raz@buildark.dev.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, reach out at raz@buildark.dev.
