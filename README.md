## Mission

To empower small businesses, growing companies, and startups with personalized, modern technology — from custom apps that streamline how they work, to polished digital presences that make them stand out. Alongside our client work, we build our own products that solve real problems for real people.

## Company Objectives

**Modernize How Businesses Operate**
Build custom applications that replace outdated, manual workflows — saving clients time, reducing errors, and giving their teams tools that actually fit the way they work.

**Elevate Every Client's Online Presence**
Design and develop landing pages and websites that look credible, feel modern, and convert visitors — whether building from scratch or transforming an existing site.

**Be the Tech Partner They Never Had**
Serve as a trusted, hands-on technology partner for businesses that lack in-house expertise — making complex solutions feel simple, approachable, and fully supported.

**Deliver Solutions That Scale**
Build with growth in mind — so the apps and sites we create today can evolve alongside our clients' businesses tomorrow.

**Build Products That Stand on Their Own**
Beyond client work, we develop and publish our own apps — one rooted in productivity, one in lifestyle — expanding our impact beyond services and into the hands of everyday users.

## Tech Stack

Pure HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript — no frameworks, no dependencies.

- Scroll-driven wave flood animation (Canvas API + `requestAnimationFrame`)
- iOS/Android optimized (`--vh` fix, DPR-aware canvas, safe-area insets)
- Responsive across mobile, tablet, and desktop
- Deployed on Vercel, source on GitHub
